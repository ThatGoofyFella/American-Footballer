Thoughts of an Atlanta Evertonian

These are exciting times around Everton Football Club. The club is without a loss in the last three matches following the appointment of Duncan Ferguson as interim manager. The club announced their new permanent manager, Carlo Ancelotti, at Saturday's match. Ancelotti has a track record of winning championships but joining Everton is unlike any project he has taken on in his career. The Blues will surely be active in the upcoming transfer window.
